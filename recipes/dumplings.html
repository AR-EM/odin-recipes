Dumplings

Description

A dumpling is a food made from dough, often cooked and eaten with soup or stew, or wrapped around a filling and baked, steamed, or fried. Dumplings are a staple in many cuisines around the world, with variations in dough and filling.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Baking Powder
  • White Sugar
  • Salt
  • Butter or Margarine
  • Milk

Steps

  1. Stir together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
  2. Cut in butter until mixture is crumbly. Stir in milk and mix until a batter forms that is thick enough to be scooped with a spoon. Allow batter to rest for 3 to 5 minutes.
  3. Drop batter by spoonfuls into boiling stew or soup. Cover and simmer without lifting the lid for 15 minutes.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
