Lassi

Description

Lassi is a yogurtâ€“based beverage with a smoothie-like consistency. It has been called "the most popular and traditional yogurt-based drink" in India.

Ingredients

  • Plain Yogurt
  • Ice Water
  • White Sugar
  • Salt
  • Crushed Ice

Steps

  1. Fill glasses with ice cubes.
  2. Place yogurt, ice water, crushed ice, sugar, and salt in a blender; blend until frothy.
  3. Pour over ice cubes in the glasses to serve.
  4. Serve and enjoy!
